## What is a LAN?

LAN stands for Local Area Network.

A LAN connects devices within a limited area such as:
- home
- office
- school
- campus

Devices inside a LAN can communicate and share resources.

---

## Advantages of a LAN

- Fast communication
- File sharing
- Internet sharing
- Device connectivity
- Resource management

---

## Network Devices

### Switch
A switch connects multiple devices within the same network.

Functions:
- forwards data
- manages communication between devices
- operates mainly using MAC addresses

---

### Router
A router connects different networks together.

Functions:
- routes traffic between networks
- connects LAN to the Internet
- assigns IP addresses in many home networks

---

## Ethernet

Ethernet is a common technology used for wired LAN communication.

Features:
- reliable
- fast
- commonly used in homes and organizations

---

## Network Topologies

Topology refers to how devices are arranged in a network.

Examples:
- Star topology
- Bus topology
- Ring topology

### Star Topology
Most common modern topology.

Characteristics:
- devices connect to a central switch/router
- easier management
- better fault isolation

---

## MAC Address

A MAC address is a unique hardware identifier assigned to a network device.

Used for:
- communication within local networks

Example:
```text
00:1A:2B:3C:4D:5E
```

---

## Key Takeaways

- LANs connect devices within small geographical areas.
- Switches and routers are essential networking devices.
- Ethernet is commonly used in LAN communication.
- Understanding LANs is important for cybersecurity and network analysis.
