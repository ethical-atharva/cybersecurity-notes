## What is Networking?

Networking is the process of connecting devices together so they can communicate and share data.

Examples:
- Computers
- Servers
- Smartphones
- Routers

---

## Why Networking is Important

Networking allows:
- Internet access
- File sharing
- Communication between systems
- Remote access
- Cloud services

Networking is essential in cybersecurity because attacks and defenses happen across networks.

---

## Types of Networks

### LAN (Local Area Network)
A network within a limited area such as:
- home
- office
- school

### WAN (Wide Area Network)
A network that covers large geographical areas.

Example:
- The Internet

---

## Networking Devices

### Router
A router connects different networks together and directs traffic.

### Switch
A switch connects devices within the same network.

---

## IP Address

An IP address identifies a device on a network.

Example:
```text
192.168.1.1
```

Types:
- Public IP
- Private IP

---

## Internet Basics

The Internet is a massive global network connecting millions of devices worldwide.

Data travels between systems using protocols and network infrastructure.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Networking is fundamental to cybersecurity.
- Devices communicate using IP addresses.
- Routers and switches are essential networking devices.
- Understanding networking is important for ethical hacking and defense.
